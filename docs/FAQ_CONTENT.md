# FAQ Content (Source of Truth)

## Getting Started
**Q: How do I join a group?**
A: Ask the group leader for an Invite Link or QR Code. Open the app and scan the QR code to join.

**Q: Can I create my own group?**
A: Yes! Go to the Groups tab and click "Create". You can make it Private (friends & family) or Public (requires staff approval).

## Contributions
**Q: How do I send money?**
A:
1. Use MoMo (*182#) to send money to your Group Treasurer's number.
2. You will receive an SMS from MoMo with a Transaction ID (TxID).
3. Open the app, go to Contribute, and enter the Amount and TxID as proof.

**Q: Why is my contribution "Pending"?**
A: The Group Treasurer needs to confirm they received the money. Allow up to 24 hours.

**Q: My submission was "Rejected".**
A: Go to Contribution History -> "Fix" to update the TxID or evidence.

## Wallet & Limits
**Q: Max savings?**
A: 500,000 RWF per wallet.

**Q: Max contribution?**
A: 4,000 RWF per transaction.
